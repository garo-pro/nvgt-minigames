Number sort
Welcome to Number Sort.

In this game, you must sort a list of numbers within whatever timeframe you set. The game is completely customizable, so that means you can change any aspect of the game to be what you want.
When you select start game, you are given a menu where you can select if you'd like to sort numbers from least to greatest or greatest to least. Select based on your preference.
After you've selected that, you are given several input boxes that will help you customize your gameplay. When you have entered all the required info, the game will start.

Press the left and right arrows to move through your numbers. Press space to pick up or put down a number.
Press t to see how much time you have left.
Finally, press escape to cancel the game.

Enjoy!
